Tips on keeping healthy hair!

  1. Do not over manipulate your hair
  2. Detangle hair from end to root
  3. Never detangle dry hair
  4. Incorporate scalp massages into your routine
  5. Twist, plait, or pineapple your hair at night to prevent matting
  6. Cover your hair at night with a satin scarf or bonnet
  7. Trim ends regularly
  8. It could be of benefit to reduce your heat usage
  9. Use non-tight protective styles such as boxbraids to tuck your hair and give it a break
  10. Determine your hair porosity for help in finding products for your hair type
